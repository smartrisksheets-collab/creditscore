Scoring engine

Rigorous. Quantitative. Built for Nigerian capital markets.

Every assessment is evaluated across four dimensions of financial health, following internationally recognised credit analysis best practice — calibrated for the Nigerian operating environment.

Liquidity analysis
Measures the issuer's ability to meet short-term obligations from liquid assets. A critical signal for instruments with tenors of 270 days or less.
Short-term coverage
Profitability metrics
Evaluates earnings quality, margin sustainability, and return on assets. Determines whether the business generates sufficient profit to support its obligations.
Earnings quality
Leverage & capital structure
Assesses total debt burden relative to assets and capital base. Identifies over-leveraged issuers whose debt capacity may be exhausted before maturity.
Debt position
Coverage & distress prediction
Scores debt serviceability from operating earnings and applies a globally recognised multi-factor distress prediction model widely used in fixed income markets.
Bankruptcy signal
